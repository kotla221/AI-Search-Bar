Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Overview

AI Search Tab is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab page with a focused, multi-engine search interface. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Do Not Collect

AI Search Tab does not:

  • Collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.
  • Track your browsing history, search queries, or activity.
  • Use analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting services.
  • display advertisements or share data with third parties.
  • Require account registration or login of any kind.

Local Storage

The extension saves three small preferences to your browser's local storage — a standard browser mechanism that keeps data on your device only and is never transmitted anywhere:

  • selectedEngine — which search engine you last used.
  • defaultEngine — your chosen default search engine.
  • theme — whether you prefer dark or light mode.

These values remain on your device and can be cleared at any time by removing the extension or clearing your browser's site data.

Permissions Explained

  • tabs — used solely to navigate the current tab to the search engine URL you selected. No tab data is read or recorded.
  • Host access to chatgpt.com and claude.ai — used only to auto-fill and auto-submit the search query you typed in the new tab page. The content script activates only when the URL contains the aisearch_submit=1 parameter that the extension itself added. No content from these pages is read, recorded, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

When you perform a search, your query is sent directly to the search engine you selected (Google, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, Reddit, X, or Amazon). Each of those services has its own privacy policy. AI Search Tab has no control over and takes no responsibility for their data practices.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
